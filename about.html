About Miclea

Built for the interview.
Not the study guide.

Most interview prep is passive. Miclea makes you practice out loud — then tells you exactly how you came across.

Our mission

Make interview practice feel like the real thing.

Interviews are a performance skill. Reading answer frameworks is like studying a sport by watching YouTube — it helps a little, but it doesn't prepare you for the pressure, the silence, or the curveball you didn't see coming.

Miclea was built to close that gap. We give you a realistic interviewer, a live microphone, and honest feedback — so by the time you walk into the real room, you've already been there.

Our goal is simple: make the best interview prep accessible to anyone, not just people who can afford an executive coach or a mock interview with a recruiter friend.

What we believe

The principles we build around — and hold ourselves to.

Practice beats theory

Knowing the STAR method and being able to use it under pressure are two completely different things. We build for the second one.

Feedback that's actually useful

Vague praise doesn't help you improve. We tell you what to fix, and why — with specific, scored feedback on clarity, structure, confidence, and relevance.

Access for everyone

A recruiter friend or an executive coach shouldn't be a prerequisite for landing a great job. We're leveling the playing field — one practice session at a time.

Honest, not easy

Real improvement comes from hard questions and honest scoring — not from an AI that tells you everything was great. We build tools that challenge you.

Our story

Started from a frustrating interview. Stayed for the mission.

Miclea started with a simple observation: the best-prepared candidates aren't always the ones who know the most — they're the ones who've said their answers out loud enough times that the words come naturally under pressure.

Traditional prep tools gave you flashcards and sample answers. We built a voice-first AI interviewer that pushes back, asks follow-ups, and scores you on how you actually came across — not just what you said.

Today, Miclea helps job seekers, bootcamp graduates, career changers, and university students prepare for the real thing. We're a small, focused team based in New York — and we care deeply about every session you take with us.
