Case study · Bootcamps

A graduation-day interview engine.

The challenge

Great engineers who froze in interviews.

Northpath's students could ship projects, but the job hunt was the bottleneck. Two career coaches couldn't run enough mock interviews for a 90-person cohort, so prep was uneven — strong students got reps, quieter ones slipped through, and placement numbers stalled at 71%.

Coaches spent their time on logistics and scheduling instead of high-value feedback.

How Miclea helped

Unlimited mocks, role-specific reps.

Northpath gave every student a Miclea seat from day one of the career phase. Students drilled on their own schedule, and coaches stepped in where the data showed it mattered.

  • Role-specific question banks for front-end, back-end, and data tracks.
  • Instructor dashboards surfaced who was practicing and who was stuck — so coaching went where it counted.
  • A weekly readiness checkpoint every student had to pass before going on the market.

The outcome

Every graduate interview-ready.

Within two cohorts, placement reached 89% and the gap between top and bottom performers shrank. Coaches reclaimed their time for mentorship, and "interview prep" became one of the program's highest-rated components.
