The Problem.

Practicing alone is a slow, unreliable way to get ready for interviews.

The Mind Blank

Real interviewers don't pause while you gather your thoughts. The pressure hits differently than any rehearsal can prepare you for.

The Knowledge Trap

Answers in your head and answers spoken aloud under scrutiny are completely different things. Reading builds knowledge — not performance.

The Generic Feedback

"You did great!" from a friend isn't coaching. You need to know exactly where you rambled, what you undersold, and what to fix.
