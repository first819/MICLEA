From ghosted to three competing offers.

The challenge

Plenty of applications, almost no callbacks.

Maya was switching careers into product management. On paper she was qualified — but after 80+ applications she had two screens and zero second rounds. When she did get in the room, behavioral questions threw her: she'd ramble, lose the thread, and walk out knowing she'd undersold herself.

Friends gave her the usual advice — "just be confident" — but nobody could tell her what was actually going wrong in her answers.

How Miclea helped

Honest reps, every single day.

Maya ran a short practice session every morning before applying. Miclea pushed back on weak answers, flagged when she rambled, and scored each response so she could see exactly where she was losing points.

  • Drilled behavioral questions with the Gauntlet until her STAR answers were tight and specific.
  • Used voice coaching to hear her own filler words and pacing — the things no friend ever mentioned.
  • Watched her Progress & Insights scores climb week over week, turning vague nerves into a clear checklist.

The outcome

Walked in already warmed up.

By the time real interviews came around, Maya wasn't fighting her nerves — she'd already faced the curveballs. Callbacks tripled, three of them turned into offers, and she negotiated her comp up with the same calm she'd rehearsed.
