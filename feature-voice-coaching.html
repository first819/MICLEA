How it works

The Gauntlet simulates the arc of a real interview — broad questions first, then harder, then a curveball — with mandatory breaks you can't click through.

01

Pick your role & set up

Three rounds, rising pressure

Round 1 is broad — background and motivation. Round 2 digs deeper into specific skills. Round 3 throws a curveball designed to test composure.

02

Mandatory 30-second breaks

Between rounds there's an enforced 30-second break you cannot skip. It's real pressure practice — just like a panel where you wait for the next interviewer.

03

Every session produces a structured score — per round, per answer, and overall — with specific feedback on what to fix before the real thing.
