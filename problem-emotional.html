The Problem.

In your head, you nailed it. Out loud, you’re a different person.

You’ll replay the interview at 2 a.m. — every filler word, every rushed answer, the point that never landed. By then it’s too late to change a thing.

Micl asks “Tell me about yourself.”
In your head

I led a team of five and shipped a product that grew revenue thirty percent in a year.

What they heard

So, um… I’ve kind of, like, done a — — — a bunch of stuff? I was sort of leading well, helping with this team, and yeah… it went pretty well, I think?
